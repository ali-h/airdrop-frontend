Airdrop FrontEnd

A front-end to use hive-engine airdrops smart contract.

Upload file or paste airdrop list below. Only Comma Seperated Values (CSV) are accepted. Example

Symbol
Distribution Type
Fee
Total Quantity
Total Accounts

Note: In order to initiate airdrop you must have BEE tokens to cover the fee. You must have enough balance of the airdrop token to cover the total quantity. If requirements are not met, your request will be declined. Enter your username and click 'Confirm' to sign transaction via Hive Keychain. After confirmation, you will be informed about the status of your request.
